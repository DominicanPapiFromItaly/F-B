Our Story

For over 40 years we have ensured operational continuity for Italian industries.

From precision mechanics to advanced maintenance: F&B is the technical partner for those who cannot afford downtime.

F&B Team Photo

The milestones of our growth

From our foundation to technical leadership in industrial construction and maintenance.

1982

The Origins

F&B was founded in 1982 by Mr. Luigi Bisogni, in an industrial context that demanded increasing levels of quality, reliability, and technical expertise.

F&B responded with a clear vision: bringing high standards of mechanical design and maintenance to a rapidly evolving market.

1980s–1990s

Technical and Industrial Growth

From the very beginning, the company stood out for its ability to handle complex issues related to machinery and industrial plants.

Special spare parts, maintenance services, and applied engineering became the pillars of its operational model.

2000–2010

Evolution of Know‑How

Decades of activity consolidated a unique technical heritage.

Special processes, certified materials, and highly qualified personnel positioned F&B as a national reference point.

Today

Presence in Strategic Sectors

F&B operates in the most demanding sectors of industrial mechanics.

The company’s solidity is demonstrated by the trust built over time.

Tomorrow

Continuous Evolution

The company continues to innovate, improve, and anticipate market needs.

Goal: delivering concrete added value, reliability, and technical vision.
